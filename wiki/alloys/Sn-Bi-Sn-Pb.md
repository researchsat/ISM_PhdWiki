# Sn-Bi and Sn-Pb alloys

**Category:** alloy

---

## Overview

Sn-Bi and Sn-Pb are historically the most-studied **binary benchmarks**
for directional solidification theory (Flemings, Glicksman, Hunt) due
to transparent X-ray contrast and low Tm. Sn-Bi is the modern, Pb-free
analogue.

## Space relevance

Peritectic couple-growth experiments on Sn-based systems in µg provided
direct tests of Hillert / Trivedi growth theories — see
[[../Observations of the occurrence and disappearance of peritectic couple growth performed under microgravity conditions_ANDREAS.pdf|Ludwig — peritectic couple growth µg]] — and the techniques transfer to
[[wiki/alloys/Pb-Sn|Pb-Sn]] and [[wiki/alloys/Sn-based|broader Sn systems]].
