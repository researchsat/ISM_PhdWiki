## Overview

Al-Si (cast alloys like A356, A357, and eutectic Al-12Si) is the second
canonical space system: irregular eutectic behaviour, strong Si
anisotropy and rich fragmentation physics. It is heavily used in
terrestrial foundries, making µg benchmarks directly translatable to
[[wiki/platforms/ISM-strategy|ISM]]-ready aluminum products.
