# Sn-based alloys

**Category:** alloy

---

## Overview

Tin-based alloys (Sn, Sn-Cu, Sn-Ag, Sn-Bi, Sn-Pb) are attractive model
systems for solidification because of low melting points and good X-ray
contrast. They appear in both eutectic and peritectic µg studies.
