## Overview

Pb-Sn is the historical workhorse for eutectic solidification theory
(Jackson-Hunt, Trivedi-Magnin-Kurz, Flemings). Despite its Pb content
it remains pedagogically central. Peritectic Pb-Bi variants share the
same class of phase topology studied in µg
([[../Observations of the occurrence and disappearance of peritectic couple growth performed under microgravity conditions_ANDREAS.pdf|Ludwig — peritectic couple growth µg]]).
