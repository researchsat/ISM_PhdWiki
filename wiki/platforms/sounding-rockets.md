# Sounding rockets (MASER, TEXUS, MAXUS)

**Category:** platform

---

## Overview

**MASER**, **TEXUS**, and **MAXUS** (ESA/SSC) sounding rockets provide
**~6–13 min of ~10⁻⁴ g**, cleaner than ISS but much shorter. Ideal for
isothermal equiaxed experiments with rapid cooling, and for testing
new X-ray diagnostics before ISS deployment.
