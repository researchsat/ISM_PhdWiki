# DECLIC-DSI (transparent alloys on ISS)

**Category:** platform

---

## Overview

**DECLIC** (Dispositif pour l'Étude de la Croissance et des Liquides
Critiques) is a CNES / NASA ISS facility whose **DSI** insert enables
long-duration directional solidification of **transparent** alloys —
principally [[wiki/alloys/SCN-transparent-analog|SCN]]-based systems. This allows real-time
optical observation of [[wiki/physics/dendritic-growth|dendrites]] and
[[wiki/physics/eutectic-solidification|eutectics]] in µg.
