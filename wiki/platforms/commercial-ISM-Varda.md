# Commercial ISM — Varda, Redwire, Space Forge

**Category:** platform

## Overview

A growing commercial tier is operationalising orbital manufacturing:

- **Varda Space Industries** — free-flying capsules returning
  pharma/materials payloads processed in µg.
- **Redwire Space** — ISS-hosted Turbine Ceramic Manufacturing Module
  (TCMM), Industrial Crystallisation Facility.
- **Space Forge** (UK) — reusable satellite "ForgeStar" for BMG and
  high-quality superalloy growth in LEO.
