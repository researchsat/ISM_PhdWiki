# ESA / CETSOL-MICAST researcher network

**Category:** author

---

## Overview

The European **CETSOL / MICAST / ICESOL** consortium is the main
network driving µg metal solidification on ISS and sounding rockets.
Core researchers include H. Nguyen-Thi, N. Bergeon, G. Reinhart,
G. Zimmermann, L. Sturz, B. Billia, D. J. Browne, and S. Akamatsu; the
[[wiki/authors/ESA-researchers|network]] also coordinates XRMON-GF and XRMON-SOL X-ray
facilities.
