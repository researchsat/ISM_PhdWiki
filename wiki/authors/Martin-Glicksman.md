# Martin Glicksman

**Category:** author

---

## Overview

**Martin Glicksman** (RPI, then FIT) pioneered the **Isothermal
Dendritic Growth Experiment (IDGE)** on the Space Shuttle — the first
quantitative µg dendrite tip data in pure SCN. His measurements
remain a touchstone for [[wiki/authors/Alain-Karma|Karma's]] thin-interface
[[wiki/modelling/phase-field|phase field]] and for current [[wiki/platforms/DECLIC-DSI|DECLIC DSI]] observations.
