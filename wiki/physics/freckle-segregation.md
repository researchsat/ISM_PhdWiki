# Freckle channels & macrosegregation

**Category:** physics

---

## Overview

**Freckles** are channel-type macrosegregation defects produced when
solute-enriched, buoyant interdendritic liquid punches upward through
the [[wiki/physics/mushy-zone|mushy zone]] zone. They are catastrophic for
[[wiki/alloys/Ni-superalloys|Ni-superalloy]] single-crystal turbine blades and are
central to [[wiki/authors/Christoph-Beckermann|Beckermann's]] double-diffusive
convection framework.

## Onset criterion

Rayleigh number in the mushy zone
**Ra = (K(gs)·β·ΔC·g·L) / (α·ν)** must exceed ≈ 0.25–0.7. On the ISS
`g → 0` so Ra → 0: freckles suppressed. On Earth or under hypergravity
([[wiki/physics/solidification|solidification]]) freckles
are promoted.

## Space twist

[[../Spurious grain formation due to Marangoni convection during directional solidification of alloys in u-g environment of International Space Station_SEYED.pdf|Seyed — Marangoni-driven spurious grains ISS]] shows Marangoni convection on ISS still
creates **spurious grains** that mimic freckle origins — so "no gravity"
≠ "no convection". Freckle-like segregation tracks can thus still appear
under [[wiki/flow/marangoni-flow|marangoni flow]].
