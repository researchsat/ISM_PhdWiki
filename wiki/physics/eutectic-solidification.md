# Eutectic solidification

**Category:** physics

---

## Overview

In a eutectic reaction L → α + β both solids grow cooperatively behind
a common front. Classical **Jackson–Hunt** theory gives
λ² · V = const and sets the spacing λ that minimises front undercooling.
In µg the **lamellar↔rod** transition and spiral eutectic patterns can
be observed without buoyant perturbations.
