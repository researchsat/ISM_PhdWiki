# Synchrotron & in-situ X-ray imaging

**Category:** method

---

## Overview

**In-situ X-ray radiography and tomography** (ESRF ID19/BM05, SPring-8,
APS, PETRA III) make the mushy zone visible in real time. The
**XRMON-GF / XRMON-SOL / XRISE-M** facilities bring this capability to
sounding rockets and soon the ISS.
