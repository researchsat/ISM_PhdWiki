# Grain refinement (inoculants, stirring, fields)

**Category:** method

---

## Overview

Grain refinement controls the final grain size — and therefore yield
strength, fatigue life, and castability — through:

1. **Inoculant particles** (Al-5Ti-1B master-alloy, TiB₂) — Greer /
   Quested free-growth criterion.
2. **Mechanical stirring / electromagnetic stirring / RMF** —
   fragmentation of dendrite arms.
3. **Ultrasonic & acoustic excitation**.
