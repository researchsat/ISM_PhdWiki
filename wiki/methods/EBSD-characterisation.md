# EBSD and post-mortem microstructural characterisation

**Category:** method

---

## Overview

**Electron Backscatter Diffraction (EBSD)** plus optical metallography
quantifies grain size, texture, and CET metrics on recovered µg
samples. Typical outputs: IPF maps, pole figures, grain-boundary
character, misorientation distributions; often coupled with EDS for
segregation maps.
